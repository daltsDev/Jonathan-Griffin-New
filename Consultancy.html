CONSULTANCY

Griff provides data-driven and innovative human performance services for athletes and teams. When it comes to improving performance, individualising our approach is key. Griff utilizes a Plan-Do-Review process which allows us to integrate a range of services aimed at providing performance support to the individual and team. The key performance pillars include mindset, movement competency, optimal physiological status, and appropriate nutrition.
