Griff provides excellent coaching process, data-supported and innovative human performance services for athletes and teams. When it comes to improving performance, individualising his approach is key. Griff utilizes an OODA-Loop process which allows him to integrate a range of services aimed at providing performance support to the individual and team. The key performance pillars include leadership & direction, development of culture & behaviours, and knowledge enhancement & sharing.
